name: utility_bill_service
description: 根据户号、月份查询水费、电费、燃气费
http_url: http://api.example.com:30000/api/utility-bill/monthly-bill
http_method: GET
parameters:
householdId: 户号，如BJ001234567，必填参数
month: 账单月份，格式YYYY-MM，必填参数
utilityType: 账单类型，可选值：electricity-电费，water-水费，gas-燃气费，默认为electricity
required: householdId,month
